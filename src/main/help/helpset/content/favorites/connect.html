Connecting to a Favorite FTP Site

To connect to an FTP site that exists in your favorites list, open the favorite manager by doing any one of the following:

  • Select Tools -> Manage Favorites... menu item from the menu bar
  • Click on the   icon in the tool bar

Doing any of the above actions will open the Manage Favorites dialog.

The Manage Favorites dialog displays a list of all your favorite FTP sites in alphabetical order. From the list, select (or highlight) the FTP site that you would like to connect to and click on the Connect button. This will close the Manage Favorites dialog and connects to the selected FTP site. Alternatively, you may double click on the site that you would like to connect to.
