Working with Favorites

One of the many features of JFTP is the ability to store the connection information of your favorite FTP sites (The FTP sites that you connect frequently). By storing this information, you don't have to enter connection information every time you want to connect to a site.

This section explains how to work with favorite FTP sites. To learn more, click on any of the sub-topics below:

  • Deleting Favorites
  • Connecting to a Favorite FTP Site
