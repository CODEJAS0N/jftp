Deleting Favorites

To delete one or more FTP sites from your favorites list, open the favorite manager by doing any one of the following:

  • Select Tools -> Manage Favorites... menu item from the menu bar
  • Click on the   icon in the tool bar

Doing any of the above actions will open the Manage Favorites dialog.

The Manage Favorites dialog displays a list of all your favorite FTP sites in alphabetical order. From the list, select (or highlight) the FTP site(s) that you would like to delete and click on the Delete button. This will open a confirmation dialog asking you if you really want to delete the selected FTP sites from your favorites. Click on the Yes button to permanently delete the selected FTP sites from your favorites. Click on the Cancel button to cancel the delete operation.
