Connecting to your Favorite FTP Site

With JFTP, connecting to your favorite FTP site is just a mouse click away. Open the favorite manager by doing any of the following:

  • Select the menu item Tools -> Manage Favorites... from the menu bar
  • Click on the   icon in the tool bar.

When you do any of the above actions, a new dialog titled, Manage Favorites will be displayed. This dialog displays all your favorite FTP sites, sorted in alphabetical order. Locate the FTP site that you would like to connect to, and double click on it. This will close the Manage Favorites dialog box and connects to the selected site. Alternatively, you can highlight the FTP site that you would like to connect to, and press the Connect button to connect to the selected site.

To quickly locate a site in the list, on your key board, press the key that matches the first letter of the FTP site's label. For example, press the key N to select the FTP site starting with the letter N .
